# ai-voice-production-workflows

## Overview
This repository outlines how I have used AI voice technology in real production environments to replace or reduce traditional voiceover workflows in client content.

The focus is on practical application rather than experimentation, specifically how voice generation can be integrated into real creative production pipelines.

---

## Tools used
- ElevenLabs
- HeyGen
- Adobe Premiere Pro
- Internal creative production workflows

---

## Use case 1: Brand explainer videos

In client work, I have used AI-generated voiceovers to replace traditional voice recording sessions for explainer content.

This allowed us to:
- Reduce production timelines
- Avoid scheduling voice talent
- Test multiple tone and delivery options quickly

---

## Use case 2: Talking head video production

We combined AI-generated voice with avatar-based video tools to create presenter-led content without filming setups.

This approach was used for internal and client-facing content where speed and iteration were more important than full studio production.

---

## Key learning

AI voice becomes most useful when treated as part of a production system rather than a standalone tool.

The value comes from:
- Faster iteration cycles
- Lower production overhead
- Flexibility in tone and delivery
